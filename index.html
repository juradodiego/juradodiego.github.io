About

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm Diego Jurado, a passionate problem solver with a fascination for the transformative power of technology in shaping our world. Currently on the cusp of graduating from the University of Pittsburgh with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, I've been on a journey fueled by curiosity and a drive to make a meaningful impact.

My path has been shaped by diverse experiences, from delving into the Ethics of AI research alongside Dr. Malihe Alikhani to immersing myself in the world of software engineering through internships. It was during my sophomore year that I became acutely aware of the ethical considerations often overlooked in the tech realm, sparking a commitment to integrating environmental consciousness and ethical principles into my work.

One pivotal moment came during my senior year when I participated in my first game jam with friends, leading to a victorious outcome with the Judge's Choice award. This experience solidified my belief in the potential of game development as a career path, alongside my enjoyment of collaborative software engineering projects.

What drives me each day is a dual motivation: the pursuit of excellence and a dedication to building a better future for my family and generations to come. Beyond coding and problem-solving, I find joy in creative outlets like reading sci-fi novels and crafting both board and video games.

Guided by principles of environmental stewardship, family-first values, and a commitment to promoting accessible education, I strive to weave these values into my professional endeavors.

Looking ahead, I envision a future where I can specialize within software engineering, potentially exploring roles in quality assurance and delving deeper into the realm of game development. On a personal level, I aspire to strike a balance between career fulfillment and ample time spent with loved ones, whether it's unwinding amidst nature's beauty or embarking on adventures around the globe.

Join me as I navigate the intersection of technology, ethics, and creativity, driven by a relentless pursuit of innovation and a heart set on leaving a positive mark on the world.
