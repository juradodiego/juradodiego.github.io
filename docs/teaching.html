Big Ideas in Computing and Information (Fall 2021)

Undergraduate Teaching Assistant

Computing and information systems underlie nearly every facet of life in today’s highly-networked societies. This course will introduce the student to a variety of core principles and important themes that cross-cut the array of computing- and information-oriented disciplines, as well as explore the types of work that individuals educated in these disciplines engage in.
